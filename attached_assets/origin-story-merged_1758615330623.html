The Crazy Idea That Worked

Then our engineer said something crazy: "What if we use THREE specialized AIs instead of one?"

We cloned their ability to #1.identify buyer intent into AI. #2.Their booking process into AI. #3.Their follow-up system into AI. Then made them work together on every single lead.
