1. Introduction

Stellah AI is a customer-conversation and appointment-management platform used by clinics, wellness businesses, and service providers.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when:

  • You use the Stellah AI platform
  • You interact with a business that uses Stellah AI
  • You visit our website or contact us

We comply with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) of Singapore and follow internationally recognised data protection principles.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

2.1 From Businesses (Clients)

  • Business information (name, contact details, account details)
  • User login and authentication data
  • Billing and subscription information
  • Support communications

2.2 From End Users Who Contact a Business Using Stellah AI

We process this data on behalf of the business:

  • Phone numbers
  • Messages sent through WhatsApp or connected channels
  • Attachments (e.g., images, documents)
  • Appointment-related information
  • Message timestamps and delivery status

This data belongs to the business you are communicating with.

2.3 Automatically Collected Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device and browser information
  • Usage and diagnostic logs

3. How We Use Personal Data

We use data to:

  • Deliver messaging and customer-service functionality
  • Route and display conversations to the business
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain and improve platform performance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or security risks
  • Comply with legal requirements

We do not use identifiable customer messages to train public AI models.

4. Legal Basis (PDPA Alignment)

We process data based on:

  • Your consent
  • The business's legitimate interests (e.g., managing their customer conversations)
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal or regulatory requirements

5. Data Protection & Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data, including:

  • Encryption in transit
  • Encryption at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure cloud hosting
  • Monitoring and audit logging

Access to data is limited to authorised personnel and used only for operational purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal data.

We may share data only with:

  • Service providers that support our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • WhatsApp / Meta as necessary for message delivery
  • Regulators or authorities if required by law

All service providers operate under confidentiality and data-protection terms.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for:

  • Delivery of services
  • Ongoing customer support
  • Legal or regulatory obligations

Businesses may request deletion of their data at any time.

8. International Transfers

If data is transferred or processed outside Singapore by our service providers, it is protected under appropriate contractual and technical safeguards.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your role:

For Businesses (Clients):

You may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction or updates
  • Data deletion
  • Withdrawal of consent

For End Users:

Please contact the business you interacted with directly, as they are the Data Controller for conversation data.

10. Links to Third-Party Services

Stellah AI integrates with WhatsApp and other third-party tools used by businesses.

We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Children's Data

Stellah AI is not intended for use by individuals under 16.

We do not knowingly collect children's data.
